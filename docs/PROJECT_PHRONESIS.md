# Project Phronesis

Project Phronesis is the engineering initiative responsible for building the PriceTrackingLLC platform.

It is not necessarily the future customer-facing product name.

## Name Origin

`Phronesis` comes from Aristotle's account of practical wisdom. In Aristotelian ethics, phronesis is not abstract knowledge alone and not technical skill alone. It is practical judgment: the capacity to deliberate well about what should be done in a real situation.

That idea fits this platform because the system is not trying to merely collect data, automate blindly, or produce opaque scores. It is trying to help a buyer make a practical decision under uncertainty.

## Relationship To Aristotle's Concept

Aristotle distinguishes practical wisdom from pure theory and craft technique. Phronesis is concerned with action: what is good to do here, with these facts, constraints, risks, and goals.

Project Phronesis adopts that posture for engineering:

- Evidence matters, but evidence is not the conclusion.
- Observations matter, but observations must be interpreted.
- Intelligence matters, but intelligence must remain explainable.
- Business decisions matter, but they must be traceable to reasons.

The platform should behave like practical judgment infrastructure: careful, evidence-aware, contextual, and transparent.

## Why Practical Judgment Fits The Platform

Trading-card buying is rarely solved by one number. A professional buyer must understand:

- What the asset is.
- Whether this workflow should evaluate it.
- Whether market evidence is fresh and relevant.
- Which provider can actually answer the question being asked.
- What the asset means for a specific business profile.
- How condition, liquidity, volatility, demand, fees, and strategy change the offer.
- Whether the correct action is BUY, NEGOTIATE, or PASS.

Project Phronesis exists to build that judgment pipeline.

The goal is practical intelligence, not price lookup. The platform should help users act wisely in a concrete buying situation.

## Engineering Identity

Project Phronesis is the engineering identity of the initiative.

It names the internal effort, philosophy, and system-building culture behind the platform. It should be used in engineering, architecture, documentation, planning, and partnership explanations when referring to the initiative that builds the evidence-driven decision intelligence platform.

It does not rename:

- The repository.
- The package.
- Imports.
- Namespaces.
- The legal entity.
- The future commercial product.

## Engineering Philosophy

Project Phronesis reflects principles already present in the architecture.

### Evidence Before Conclusions

The platform should gather, validate, classify, and preserve evidence before making claims. Unknown evidence should reduce confidence rather than create false certainty.

### Explainable Intelligence

Intelligence outputs should expose reasons, signals, confidence, supporting evidence, and limitations. Opaque scoring is not enough.

### Observations Separated From Reasoning

Providers contribute observations. The platform interprets those observations. Raw provider data should not become business truth without normalization, ontology, validation, evidence layering, and reasoning.

### Providers Contribute Observations

Provider integrations should answer what they are actually capable of answering. The Market Ontology prevents providers from being used outside their evidence domain.

### The Platform Produces Intelligence

The platform derives Market Intelligence, Asset Assessment, negotiation guidance, and decision output from validated evidence and business context. It should not merely relay provider fields.

### Business Decisions Remain Transparent

BUY, NEGOTIATE, and PASS should remain traceable to market evidence, asset assessment, business profile assumptions, strategy, and negotiation logic.

### Judgment Over Automation

Automation should support judgment, not erase it. The platform should help users decide, not pretend that every situation can be collapsed into an unexplained answer.

### Practical Intelligence Over Opaque Scoring

Scores can be useful, but only when paired with reasons and evidence. Project Phronesis prioritizes practical intelligence that can be inspected, challenged, and improved.
