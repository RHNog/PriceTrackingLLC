## 2. One-Sentence Vision

**Confidence precedes commerce.**

---

## 3. Executive Summary

**This is a partnership conversation, not an API access request.**

Project Phronesis is the internal engineering initiative responsible for developing an evidence-driven decision intelligence platform for collectible markets.

It is the engineering identity, not necessarily the future customer-facing product name.

The platform helps collectors, vendors, and professional operators make better decisions from available market evidence.

---

## 4. Why Project Phronesis Exists

**The platform is not designed merely to display market data.**

It exists to help users make better decisions.

Practical wisdom means:

- Evidence before conclusions.
- Disciplined judgment before action.
- Explainable intelligence instead of opaque claims.
- Marketplace enablement rather than marketplace replacement.

---

## 5. Why This Benefits TCGplayer

**Decision support strengthens marketplaces.**

Project Phronesis can help marketplace participants become:

- More confident before purchase.
- More informed before restock.
- More prepared to act.
- More likely to reach trusted marketplaces with clear intent.

---

## 9. Responsible Platform Philosophy

**Responsible integration means respecting partner infrastructure.**

Responsible platform principles:

- Reuse previously generated observations where appropriate.
- Avoid unnecessary repeated requests.
- Request external data only when there is a workflow reason.
- Keep information boundaries clear.
- Respect marketplace and provider infrastructure.

---

## 10. Affiliate Alignment

**Affiliate participation should align incentives, not imply access or endorsement.**

If Project Phronesis helps users evaluate assets and reach purchase intent, affiliate pathways could direct qualified, purchase-ready users toward TCGplayer.

Alignment:

- Users receive decision support.
- TCGplayer receives higher-intent traffic.
- Sellers benefit from more informed buyers.
- Project Phronesis supports marketplace activity without becoming a marketplace.
