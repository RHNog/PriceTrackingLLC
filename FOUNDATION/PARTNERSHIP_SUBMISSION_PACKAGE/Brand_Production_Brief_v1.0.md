# Project Phronesis Brand Production Brief v1.0

## Project Overview

Project Phronesis is the internal engineering initiative responsible for developing an evidence-driven decision intelligence platform for collectible markets.

The commercial product name remains intentionally undecided. The brand work should support Project Phronesis as an institutional engineering identity, not force a final customer-facing product name.

## Brand Philosophy

Project Phronesis is built around practical wisdom: the disciplined ability to make sound decisions under real-world conditions.

The brand should communicate:

- Evidence before conclusions.
- Explainability over opacity.
- Judgment before automation.
- Trust before growth.
- Marketplace enablement.
- Long-term discipline.

## Meaning Of Phronesis

`Phronesis` comes from Aristotle's concept of practical wisdom.

It is not abstract theory and not technical skill alone. It is judgment applied to action in concrete, imperfect, consequential situations.

The platform should feel like practical judgment infrastructure.

## Brand Positioning

Project Phronesis is:

- An evidence-driven decision intelligence platform.
- A practical intelligence layer for collectible markets.
- A system for turning observations into explainable judgment.

Project Phronesis is not:

- A marketplace.
- A scraper.
- A bot.
- A price manipulation platform.
- An opaque scoring product.

## Compass Concept

The preferred identity concept is a restrained compass.

The compass should suggest judgment, orientation, trust, and direction without looking nautical, fantasy-themed, ornamental, or overdesigned.

The compass should feel institutional and precise.

## Approved Tagline

**Practical Wisdom. Better Decisions.**

## Typography

| Role | Typeface |
| --- | --- |
| Headings | Cinzel |
| Body | Inter |

Typography should feel premium but readable. Avoid negative letter spacing, novelty typefaces, or decorative display usage that weakens trust.

## Design Principles

- Clarity over impressiveness.
- Restraint over decoration.
- Whitespace over clutter.
- Evidence over hype.
- Institutional calm over trend-driven visuals.
- One primary idea per page or slide.

## Deliverables

Designer or production deliverables should include:

- Primary raster logo.
- Vector logo.
- Horizontal lockup.
- Icon-only mark.
- Light background version.
- Dark background version.
- Brand board.
- Color tokens.
- Typography guidance.
- Presentation cover example.
- Document header/footer example.

## Brand Guidelines

The logo should not be stretched, distorted, recolored outside the approved palette, placed on low-contrast backgrounds, or surrounded by decorative effects.

The identity should be serious enough for investors, marketplace partners, and engineering documentation.

## Success Criteria

The finished brand system should:

- Communicate practical wisdom.
- Feel premium, minimal, and timeless.
- Work in documents, decks, and product-adjacent contexts.
- Avoid startup noise and speculative visual language.
- Preserve trust and clarity.
- Allow future vector replacement without layout redesign.

## Designer Instructions

Design for a serious engineering organization intended to exist for decades.

Do not optimize for decoration. Optimize for trust, comprehension, and durability.

The reader should understand that Project Phronesis is disciplined, evidence-driven, and built around practical judgment.
