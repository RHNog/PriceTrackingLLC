# Executive Deck Template

Use this template for executive partnership, investor, and strategic presentation decks.

## Structure

1. Cover
2. One-Sentence Vision
3. Executive Summary
4. Why Project Phronesis Exists
5. Why This Benefits The Audience
6. Ecosystem Flywheel
7. Market Or Regional Vision
8. Future User Journeys
9. Responsible Platform Philosophy
10. Incentive Alignment
11. What We Are Not
12. Long-Term Partnership Or Strategic Vision
13. Call To Conversation
14. Platform Snapshot
15. Closing

## Rules

- One primary idea per slide.
- Every major slide begins with one memorable sentence.
- Use diagrams only when they reduce reading effort.
- Avoid dense tables.
- Avoid clipart and decorative illustration.
- Preserve current capability vs future vision.
- Use Deep Navy, Gold, Slate, White, and Off White.
- Use Cinzel for identity headings and Inter for readable body text.
