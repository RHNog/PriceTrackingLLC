# Project Phronesis Brand Book

Project Phronesis is the engineering identity behind an evidence-driven decision intelligence platform for collectible markets.

The commercial product name remains intentionally undecided. This brand book governs the Project Phronesis identity as an internal engineering initiative, partnership-facing philosophy, and institutional standard.

## Brand Essence

Project Phronesis stands for practical wisdom.

It is calm, evidence-based, disciplined, and built for serious decisions under uncertainty.

## Positioning

Project Phronesis is not a price tracker, marketplace, scraper, bot, or opaque scoring product.

It is practical decision intelligence for collectible markets.

## Core Beliefs

- Evidence before conclusions.
- Explainability over opacity.
- Judgment before automation.
- Platforms over features.
- Long-term thinking.
- Responsible partnerships.
- Marketplace enablement.
- Trust before growth.

## Voice

Project Phronesis communicates with restraint.

The voice should be:

- Professional.
- Executive.
- Honest.
- Precise.
- Calm.
- Defensible.

Avoid hype, exaggerated maturity, unexplained superlatives, and speculative claims presented as current capability.

## Visual Identity

The visual system should look the way the organization thinks:

- Minimal.
- Elegant.
- Premium.
- Timeless.
- Clear.
- Trustworthy.

Use whitespace as a comprehension tool. Use diagrams only when they clarify relationships. Use color as a restrained signal, not decoration.

## Color Palette

| Token | Hex | Role |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Deep Navy | `#0B1F33` | Primary identity, headings, trusted emphasis |
| Gold | `#C6A15B` | Accent, dividers, restrained institutional signal |
| Slate | `#5D6B7A` | Secondary text and metadata |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Primary canvas |
| Off White | `#F7F4EE` | Soft panels and callouts |

## Typography

| Use | Typeface |
| --- | --- |
| Institutional headings | Cinzel |
| Body and operational text | Inter |

Fallbacks should preserve readability and restraint.

## Tagline

Approved tagline:

**Practical Wisdom. Better Decisions.**

## Logo

Use the approved raster Project Phronesis logo when available.

Until the final vector logo exists, layouts should reserve a stable logo area so replacement requires changing one image asset, not redesigning documents.

## Communication Standard

Every communication should answer:

- What do we believe?
- Why does it matter?
- What exists now?
- What is future vision?
- What are we asking the reader to do?
- What are we not claiming?
