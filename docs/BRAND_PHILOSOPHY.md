# Brand Philosophy

This document records brand philosophy without selecting a final commercial product name.

## Current Identity Position

The platform currently has three separate identity layers:

```text
Legal Entity
  -> PriceTrackingLLC

Future Commercial Product
  -> intentionally undecided

Engineering Initiative
  -> Project Phronesis
```

These identities should evolve independently.

Project Phronesis gives the engineering work a durable philosophical identity. It does not force a customer-facing brand decision.

## Project Phronesis As Engineering Identity

Project Phronesis names the internal engineering initiative building the evidence-driven decision intelligence platform for collectible markets.

It signals:

- Practical judgment.
- Evidence-driven reasoning.
- Explainable intelligence.
- Transparent decisions.
- Human agency supported by software.
- Discipline around provider observations, market evidence, and business conclusions.

## Commercial Product Name

The commercial product name remains intentionally undecided.

Future brand evaluation should consider:

- Memorability.
- Spellability.
- Trust.
- Category clarity.
- Professional buyer appeal.
- Store operator appeal.
- Expansion beyond trading cards into collectible markets.
- Compatibility with AI assistant, API, inventory, and portfolio surfaces.

Project Phronesis may remain internal even if the commercial product launches under another name.

## Brand Principles

### Judgment, Not Hype

The brand should communicate sound decision support rather than speculative hype. The platform is for operators making real buying decisions.

### Evidence, Not Guesswork

The brand should emphasize traceable evidence, provider discipline, and clear reasoning.

### Practical Intelligence, Not Mystery Scores

The platform can use scores, grades, and confidence labels, but the brand should not lean on opaque magic. It should make intelligence feel inspectable and usable.

### Professional Calm

The tone should feel serious, practical, and operational. The platform should earn trust from people who use it repeatedly under time pressure.

### Human Decision Ownership

The platform supports human judgment. It should not present itself as replacing the buyer, store owner, or operator.

## Messaging Themes

Possible themes:

- Decision intelligence for collectible markets.
- Evidence-driven buying decisions.
- Practical market intelligence for professional card buyers.
- Turn observations into judgment.
- Explainable intelligence for high-volume buying workflows.
- A decision operating system for collectible assets.

These are themes, not final taglines.

## Partnership Guidance

When writing future partnership documents:

- Use `Project Phronesis (Engineering Initiative)` for the internal build effort.
- Describe it as the internal engineering initiative responsible for building an evidence-driven decision intelligence platform for collectible markets.
- Do not present Project Phronesis as the commercial brand.
- Do not imply the future product name has been selected.
- Preserve PriceTrackingLLC as the current legal or organizational reference where appropriate.

Suggested phrasing:

> Project Phronesis is the internal engineering initiative behind PriceTrackingLLC's evidence-driven decision intelligence platform for collectible markets.

Avoid:

> Project Phronesis is the customer-facing product brand.

## Naming Guardrails

Do not rename:

- Repository.
- Package.
- Application title.
- Imports.
- Namespaces.
- GitHub assets.
- Existing historical documentation.

Introduce Project Phronesis only as an engineering identity and philosophy.

## Open Brand Questions

- Should the commercial product name be literal and category-clear or philosophical and ownable?
- Should the future brand focus on trading cards first or collectible markets broadly?
- Should the AI assistant, if launched, have a separate name?
- Should store plans and professional plans share the same product brand?
- Should API or white-label offerings use the same commercial brand?
- How much of the Phronesis philosophy should be visible to customers?
